V1 · Live Buy Feed — Bandwagon & Zero-Friction
Strategy: Show live buys happening NOW with an inline Buy button on every row. "Bought $500 of PEPE, 2m ago" with a green Buy pill = zero distance from seeing to acting. "342 people buying right now" hero creates urgency. Dollar amounts ($500, $1,200, $2,000) anchor what's normal to spend. "4 others buying" badges trigger bandwagon effect. Buy pill opens a token-specific sheet with Apple Pay for zero-friction purchase. Best for: Action-oriented traders who buy on impulse.
V2 · The Shield — Safety & Loss Aversion
Strategy: Lead with protection, not opportunity. "Your first trade will be protected" reframes Matcha as the safe choice. The $412 vs $62 comparison (unprotected vs SL/TP) makes the cost of NOT using Matcha visceral. The checklist creates trust. Best for: Burned traders who've held bags and want guardrails.
V3 · Simulated Trade — Tangibility & Clarity
Strategy: Show, don't tell. A realistic trade card with sparkline + SL/TP lines makes the abstract concept of stop-loss/take-profit visually concrete. The user can SEE what a protected position looks like before depositing. Best for: Visual learners and first-time traders who need to understand the product.
V4 · The Race — Competition & Urgency
Strategy: Leverage social comparison. A live leaderboard of top savers creates competitive urgency ("others are ahead of you"). "Where do you rank?" turns the regret calculator into a ranking tool. "Next wave forming" with buy counts creates time pressure. Best for: Competitive, active traders who respond to FOMO and rankings.
